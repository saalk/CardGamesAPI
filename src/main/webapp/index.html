Welcome alien!

and play this simple, but yet very addictive High-low card game

This game is an angular powered, skeleton css based java application that runs on any device. Choose amongst the many java back-end implemented options while playing this High-Low game. Start with bailing your ship and get a random starting amount up to 1000 credits!

Select up to 2 ai Players to join you; we have them with an ai level of high, average or low. All you do is guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the card facing up.
See how many you can get in a row. Only after one guess you can opt to pass on to the next player or keep playing for a next turn.
Win or lose depends on a series of good guesses and of course the result of your selected opponent ai player(s) strenght...
