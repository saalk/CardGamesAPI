and play this simple, but yet very addictive High-low card game

Like Netflix and YouTube this web application is build with directives and views supplied by the popular framework AngularJS. Being a Single Page app this site is a showcase for responsive webdesign thanks to the 'death simple' css framework Skeleton.

So choose amongst the many game options while playing this High-Low card game. Start to bail your ship and receive a (randomly generated) starting amount of up to 1000 credits!

Select up to 2 AI opponents to join you; we have them with various ai levels hard, moderate and easy. All you do is guess if the next card will be higher or lower than the card facing up. See how many you can get in a row!

Only after one guess you can opt to pass on to the next player, but keep playing next turn will reward you double or nothing.
Win or lose depends on a series of good guesses and of course the result of your selected opponent ai player(s) strength...
